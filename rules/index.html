Tournament structure

The FIFA World Cup 2026 has 48 teams in 12 groups of 4. The group stage produces 72 matches. 32 teams advance to the knockout phase: the 12 group winners, 12 runners-up, and the 8 best third-placed teams. The bracket runs Round of 32 → Round of 16 → Quarter-Finals → Semi-Finals → Final, plus a 3rd-place play-off.

Tournament window: 11 Jun — 19 Jul 2026.

Knockout scoring is per-team-in-round

A team predicted to reach the Quarter-Finals scores its 15 points if it gets there via any bracket path. The opponent or specific R32 match does not matter — only whether the team appears in that round.
