How to enter

Everything you need to download the prediction sheet, fill it in correctly, and submit it before kickoff. Looking for how points are awarded instead? See the Rules page.

Deadline: entries lock at kickoff — 11 Jun 2026, 16:00 UTC. Nothing submitted after that can be scored.

1. Get the sheet

Download the blank entry sheet, then open it in Excel (or Google Sheets / LibreOffice).

⬇ Download the entry sheet (.xlsx)

2. Fill it in

Only the highlighted (yellow) input cells are yours to fill. Here's what each section needs:

  • Your details — type your name, email, and a public handle in the boxes at the top right of the sheet. The handle is the name shown on the public leaderboard; your email is how you receive your personal results link (it is never published).
  • Group stage — for every group match across Groups A–L, predict the home score and away score. There are 72 group matches; fill the score boxes next to each fixture.
  • Knockout picks — name the teams you think reach each round: Round of 32 (32 teams), Round of 16 (16), Quarter-Finals (8), Semi-Finals (4), and the Final (2) — plus your overall Winner.
    Knockout points are awarded per team reaching a round, independent of the bracket path — a team you place in the Quarter-Finals scores whether it gets there via any matchup. You don't need to predict specific fixtures, just which teams go that far. (scoring details.)
  • Tie-breaker — predict the total number of goals scored across all 104 matches, including extra time but excluding penalty shoot-outs. If two players tie on points, closest to the actual total wins.

3. Don't change the template structure

Fill in the input cells only. Do not rename, move, delete, or reorder the team names, group headers, or any locked label cells. The ingester verifies a "template signature" — a set of fixed cells that must stay exactly as shipped — and will reject your file if the structure has been edited.

Typos in a team name you type (e.g. "Brasil" for "Brazil") are fine — those are fuzzy-matched automatically. What breaks ingestion is altering the template's own layout or pre-printed labels.
